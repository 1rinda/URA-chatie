Importing Shoes to Uganda

Official procedure for commercial import of footwear via Uganda Revenue Authority.

1

Pre-clearance

expand_more

Submit required documentation including pro-forma invoices and certificates of origin to the relevant authorities for verification before shipment arrives. Ensure all shoes meet UNBS quality standards.

Required: E-Invoice 3 Days avg.
2

Declaration

expand_more

Lodgement of the customs declaration in the ASYCUDA World system. This includes specifying the HS Codes for shoes and calculating applicable taxes based on the CIF value.

VIEW REQUIRED DOCUMENTS open_in_new
3

Payment

expand_more

Payment of assessed duties, VAT, and other levies through authorized commercial banks or mobile money platforms.

Estimated Tax Rate

25% Import Duty + 18% VAT

4

Release

expand_more

Once payment is confirmed, the system generates a release order. Goods can then be picked up from the customs bonded warehouse.
