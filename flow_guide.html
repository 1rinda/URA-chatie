Clearance of Imports at Malaba

smart_toy

Welcome to the Malaba Customs Assistant. Below is the comprehensive step-by-step procedural guide for clearing imported goods at the Malaba border post:

1

Document Verification

Submit original commercial invoices, Bill of Lading, and Packing List to the customs desk for initial validity check.

2

Declaration Lodgment

Log into the Single Window Electronic System (SIMBA/ASYCUDA) to lodge your import declaration and generate a payment slip.

3

Payment of Duties

Proceed to an authorized partner bank to pay all assessed taxes, duties, and regional levies as per the electronic declaration.

4

Physical Inspection

Customs officials will perform a joint physical verification of goods to ensure consistency with the declared documentation.

5

Final Release

Upon successful verification, a release order is issued. The truck is then cleared to exit the customs yard and enter the destination territory.
